Privacy Policy for StellaBridge

Effective Date: July 7, 2026

StellaBridge ("the App") is a school management and communication application developed by Vishnu Shaji. The App is intended for use by authorized school administrators, teachers, parents, and students.

Information We Collect

Depending on your role and use of the App, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Mobile number
  • Login credentials
  • Student academic information
  • Attendance records
  • Homework and assignment information
  • Chat messages
  • Images uploaded within the App
  • Audio recordings submitted for assignments
  • Profile photographs (if provided)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate users.
  • Provide school management services.
  • Facilitate communication between administrators, teachers, parents, and students.
  • Manage attendance and academic records.
  • Deliver homework and assignments.
  • Store submitted images and audio files.
  • Improve the functionality and security of the App.

Data Storage

User data is securely stored using Supabase cloud services. Uploaded media such as images, homework attachments, and audio recordings may be automatically removed after a period of time to manage storage capacity, where appropriate.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information.

User information is shared only as necessary to provide the App's educational services, including displaying relevant information to authorized school staff, parents, and students.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information. Data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using secure HTTPS connections.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by students under the supervision of their school and parents. Schools are responsible for authorizing student accounts and managing access.

Account Management

User accounts are created and managed by the school administration. Users cannot independently register within the App.

Data Retention

We retain user information only for as long as necessary to provide educational services, comply with school requirements, and meet applicable legal obligations.

Some temporary content, such as uploaded media, may be automatically deleted after a retention period.

Requesting Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and personal data by following the instructions provided on the StellaBridge Data Deletion page.

Certain records, including academic records, attendance history, or other information that schools are required to retain, may be kept where required by applicable laws or school policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

Developer: Vishnu Shaji

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact the school administration or the contact details provided on the application's official website.

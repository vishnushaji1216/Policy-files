StellaBridge – Account & Data Deletion Request

If you would like your StellaBridge account or personal data to be deleted, you may submit a deletion request through your school administration.

Important: StellaBridge accounts are created and managed by the school administration. Users cannot delete their own accounts directly.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Contact your school administration or the authorized StellaBridge administrator.
  2. Provide your registered mobile number and your full name for verification.
  3. Clearly state that you wish to delete your StellaBridge account and associated personal data.

What Will Be Deleted

Where applicable, the following information will be permanently deleted:

  • User account
  • Login credentials
  • Chat messages
  • Uploaded images
  • Uploaded audio recordings
  • Profile photograph
  • Other personal information associated with the account, unless retention is required.

Information That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained where required by applicable laws, school regulations, or legitimate administrative purposes, including:

  • Academic records
  • Attendance records
  • Fee and payment records
  • Administrative audit logs

Any retained information will only be kept for educational, legal, regulatory, or administrative purposes.

Processing Time

Eligible deletion requests are generally processed within 30 days after successful verification.
